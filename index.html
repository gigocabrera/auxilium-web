About Me

Hello! i'm Luis Cabrera

I am a family man and a dedicated dad who believes that family always comes first. I am an Applications Architect by day and an ionic framework and firebase addict by night. Aside from technology and family, I truly enjoy the outdoors and working on our horse farm.

I was born and raised in Guatemala, Central America, and came to the United States during the dot-com bubble in August of 2000 after being hired by a start-up (dot-com) company. So after selling everything we owned in Guatemala, my wife and I moved to Florida with our 3 kids and 2 dogs and two months later the company went south and it closed it’s doors. That’s when I learned the hard way that there was a dot-com bubble and it had burst. Imagine the look in my wife’s face when I broke the news. Yeah…

But no complains after that. Life has been taking care of us (with lots of cuts, countless bruises and bleeding noses, that is…)
